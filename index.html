Biography

Hello, my name is Eric Puigmarti. I am a multidisciplinary designer based in Toronto, Canada. I fell into design before even knowing that design was. As a kid, I admired illustration and graphic design of my favourite band’s album artwork. I’m not just passionate about visual design but about the entire process of building digital products. After studying design in college,. From ideation, sketching, prototyping, user testing and development, each has equal value and impact into the project's success.

I work on a team with super talented people at Heist to design beautiful experiences for digital products. I am not the type of person to stand still. I enjoy keeping myself busy and learning humbly.

    Shifthub

    Challenge:

    For years, small businesses have struggled to access the right tools to manage their organizations digitally. The three largest pains for most small businesses are scheduling, communication and distributing important company information.


    Employees continue to feel disconnected to their organization and often need to diligently check their emails or call managers to find out when they're working next.


    There is huge demand for small businesses to stay connect with their employees. Shifthub closes the communication gap between managers and employees allowing for easy scheduling, group messaging, and payroll management tools.
