About

Hello! I am a designer based in Toronto, Canada. I fell in love with design before I understood that design was a thing. As a kid, I was always interested in arts and design as a hobby.

Today, I focus on Interface Design, Interaction Design, and User Experience Design. I work on a team of incredibly talented people, at Freshbooks, where we help small businesses make sense of accounting. I am not the type of person to stand still, I enjoy keeping myself busy while learning humbly. Designing successful products means observing how real people solve problems now, exploring the context of the situation they are in, and then understanding causality, anxieties and, motivations.

Shifthub for iOS

Shifthub

Challenge:

For years, small businesses have struggled to access the right tools to manage their organizations digitally. The three largest pains for most small businesses are scheduling, communication and distributing important company information. Employees continue to feel disconnected to their organization and often need to diligently check their emails or call managers to find out when they're working next.


There is huge demand for small businesses to stay connect with their employees. Shifthub closes the communication gap between managers and employees allowing for easy scheduling, group messaging, and payroll management tools.

Koge Vitamins

Koge is a Toronto-based vitamin company founded on the premise that buying vitamins shouldn’t be complicated or cost a fortune. Koge provides premium quality vitamins in goal-oriented packs and delivers them to your door every month.

Challenge:
The goal of these product pages are to educate potential consumers about the benefits and difference between each of Koge's vitamin bundles. Each vitamin pack that Koge offers are centered around health goals. While designing wireframes, I discovered that it would be necessary to design several components that would scale based on the different bundle that Koge offers.
