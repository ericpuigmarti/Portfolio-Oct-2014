Eric is a Product Designer in Toronto. Currently, he is building digital products at FreshBooks. He loves products that are meaningful and strive to make our lives, as humans, better.

Building Products that matter.

Love

    Hello.

    As a kid, Eric fell in love with design and technology before he even knew what they were. His love of music, technology and the arts drew him to pursue his passion and education in designing to effect positive change.

    Today, Eric focuses on Design Research, Interface Design, and User Experience Design. Designing successful products means observing how real people solve problems, exploring the context and situations they encounter, and understanding their needs, anxieties and, motivations.
